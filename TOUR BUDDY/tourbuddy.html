Most Popular Tours

Vrindavan

Vrindavan is a city in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. It's located on the west bank of the Yamuna River, about 15 kilometers north of Mathura.

Shimla

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh, a state in northwest India. It is located on a ridge of the Himalayan foothills, at an elevation of about 7,100 feet (2,200 meters) northeast of Chandigarh

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state in northern India and the largest state in the country by area. It's also known as the "Land of Kings" or "Land of Kingdoms".

Goa

Goa is a state in southwestern India, known as the "Pearl of the Orient". It's India's smallest state, with a total area of 1,430 square miles.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a state in northern India. It was formed in 2000 from the northern part of Uttar Pradesh. The state's name comes from the Sanskrit words uttara (north) and khaṇḍa (land)

Kashmir

Kashmir is a region in the northwestern part of the Indian subcontinent. It is bordered by China to the northeast and east, India to the south, Pakistan to the west, and Afghanistan to the northwest.
